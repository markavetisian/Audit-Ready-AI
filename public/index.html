Understanding SOC 2

What is SOC 2?
SOC 2 is a security framework defined by the AICPA for SaaS companies. A SOC 2 report is issued by a licensed CPA firm after an independent audit.

Type 1 vs Type 2
Type 1: Point-in-time audit — "Controls are designed appropriately as of [date]."
Type 2: Period audit (6-12 months) — "Controls operated effectively over [period]."
Most enterprise customers require Type 2.

What AuditReady does
AuditReady helps you prepare for a SOC 2 audit. It tracks your evidence, identifies gaps, and generates AI-assisted readiness reports. It does NOT replace a licensed CPA audit.

Getting a Real Audit
Once your AuditReady score reaches 80%+, you are typically ready to engage a CPA firm. Common auditors: Schellman, Johanson Group, Prescient Assurance, A-LIGN.

⚡ Tip: AuditReady aims to get you audit-ready in 30-60 days, cutting 2-3 months off the typical preparation timeline.
